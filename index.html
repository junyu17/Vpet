Privacy Policy

VirtualPets: Pet Care World · Last updated: July 24, 2026

Summary: VirtualPets: Pet Care World does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game progress is saved only on your own device.

Data collection

This app does not collect any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers. You are not required to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

The app does not access advertising identifiers (IDFA), the vendor identifier, location, camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, calendar, or health data, and it makes no network requests of its own.

Data storage

All game data — including your pets, points, and progress — is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to us or to any third party. This data is removed when you delete the app.

Third-party services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in this app. The app has no third-party software dependencies and uses only Apple's own system frameworks.

Notifications

The app may ask your permission to send reminder notifications, such as letting you know your pet needs care. These reminders are scheduled entirely on your own device. There is no push notification server, no device token is created or sent, and no information is collected or transmitted in order to deliver them. You may decline this permission, or turn it off at any time in the Settings app, and the rest of the app will continue to work normally.

In-app purchases

The app offers one optional in-app purchase. That transaction is processed entirely by Apple through your App Store account, and we do not receive or store your payment information. Before the purchase screen appears, the app shows a parental gate — a question intended for an adult — so that a child cannot make a purchase without a parent or guardian present.

Children's privacy

This app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for children. Because no personal data is collected from any user, no personal data is collected from children either. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose any personal information from children, and the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and with the children's provisions of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K). There is no advertising, no behavioral profiling, no analytics, and no social or messaging feature through which a child could share personal information.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
