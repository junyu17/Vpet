Privacy Policy

VirtualPets: Pet Care World · Last updated: July 15, 2026

Summary: VirtualPets: Pet Care World does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game progress is saved only on your own device.

Data collection

This app does not collect any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers. You are not required to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

Data storage

All game data — including your pets, points, and progress — is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to us or to any third party.

Third-party services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in this app.

In-app purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, that transaction is processed entirely by Apple through your App Store account. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's privacy

This app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, no personal data is collected from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at billy.yu@me.com.
